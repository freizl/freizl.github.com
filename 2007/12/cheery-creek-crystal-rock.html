Cheery Creek & Castle Rock

2007-12-29
InUS

It’s weekend. So it’s shopping day as some guys will go back to China soon.
We started to Cheery Creek first which has lots of cosmetics, perfume, clothing, electronic, etc..
We saw Clinique, Lascome, Channel and many brands we didn’t know ever.
There’s a Apple store as well and we tested i Phone. How cool it is! 8G memory, 399$ without tax.
The only disappointed thing is I can’t use it in China as well as in US.

My partners bought something then we went to Castle Rock. It’s a shopping place. A lot of clothing stores.
I only know several brands here. Many products are on sale and I got one item at Timberland.

Apparently I need to know more about those two place by search on internet in case I will not lost when going next time.
