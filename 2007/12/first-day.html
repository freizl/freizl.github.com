The first day

2007-12-27
InUS

It’s snow day here.
We walked for about 10 Minutes to take bus to client office and learned the
environment.
Everywhere is covered by snow. The view outside is like what we saw in
some old US films.
And the bus is definitely more comfortable than that in Hangzhou as
well as it’s bigger.

About half of ten o’clock, Billy brought our new arrivals to
our company office. It’s
smaller than the GDC office and nothing better than GDC I think. Lots
of people are not here since now it’s the holiday season and most of
are on vocation.

It took us more than 15 Min. to a market to buy something. And it’s
terrible walking in the snow.
The market is not very big but it has almost all what we want. And
what can I say, all the thing was labeled in English and I’m lost most
of the time.
And the 16th ST. is the *main line* here. Many stores at this street.
Back to office around 2PM and checking the mail and going back to
hotel after 1 hour because the snow will be worse later.

One thing I got strong feeling is the English skills is a big problem
and also a big challenge if I want to live here comfortable.
