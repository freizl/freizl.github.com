Start

2007-03-03

As I used several google services more and more these days, such as mail,reader,bookmark,book note,
so I start to adopt its blog service Blogger instead of live spaces which I think has a more beautiful displaying.

Blogger was unprohibited by Chinese Government last year and also it was updated to a better version, more easy use.
Blogger has a pretty interesting feature that one account can create multi blog sites,not only one.
For instance I create another two blog site, freizl2.blogspot.com and freizlnews.blogspot.com.
Is seems other blog services do not have this feature.
And people also can custom blog layout through editing HTML code.Isn’t it a cool feature for a advance blog user?

I am not sure what I want to blog here but I might blog more frequently than before.
And it’s so terrible and shameful thing English is still a bigger challenge now than technology.
