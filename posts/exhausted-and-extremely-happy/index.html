Exhausted and extremely happy

2007-10-02
Life

It’s great interesting to get a comment from a foreigner on previous Chinese post.
Maybe he’s interested in foreign language like Chinese, or something else.
And I have to say I’m not able to write every post in English just because of my little English skills.
I couldn’t express something in my mind exactly in English and mostly English is used for reporting something and instant communication.

On the Oct. 01 and Oct. 02, first two days of our National Holiday, we enjoyed every second.
Hiking on the Longgangshan Mountain and Yandangshan Mountain, parting, KTV, sleeping, mah-jong, volleyball, badminton.
We’re pretty sorry that we forgot to take along a digit camera. So we didn’t take anything photos.
Maybe I will share most of the details later on.
