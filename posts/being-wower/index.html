    Being a WOWer

    2007-06-09
    Life

    I came back to the World of Warcraft after several months.
    There was a new expansion the Burning crusade having lots of new features.
    Two new races, new land named Outland,the highest level now is 70th,etc.
    Those all seemed to be very interesting.
    But after I launched the game and played for several levels, I found it’s almost the same as before.
    Even new tasks,the process is identical. I mean in the lower level because I don’t what would happen after 60th level.
    It seems that we have to duplicate what we had done many times from about 20th level to 60th.
    Probably It would be boring.

    Persist is being a problem to me again. I wanted to give up learning English.
    I couldn’t find I made a progress in English skills after more than one month learning.
    So why was that. I though the most important thing I forgot is reciting.
    I should recite words ,expression and even articles. Learning these things by heart.
